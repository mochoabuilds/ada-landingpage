Our History

The story begins in 2018 along the Chicago riverwalk. I (Michael Valentino Ochoa) was considering graduate studies and the riverway was a frequent spot for my C++/math self study before my work shifts. By some accounts, the sounds of the river struck me first. I grew interested in the sounds made as the wake splashed the riverwalk due to a passing boat. Soon after, half formed ideas began to coalesce. Working from my apartment, I built a prototype scale model water robotaxi that seeks to travel from the Chicago Riverwalk to Goose Island without human intervention.
